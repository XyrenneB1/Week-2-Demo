Scientific Information about Penguins

Taxonomy

The family name of Spheniscidae was given by Charles Lucien Bonaparte from the genus Spheniscus,[24] the name of that genus comes from the Greek word σφήν sphēn "wedge" used for the shape of an African penguin's swimming flippers.[25]

Evolution

Although the evolutionary and biogeographic history of Sphenisciformes is well-researched, many prehistoric forms are not fully described. Some seminal articles about the evolutionary history of penguins have been published since 2005.
